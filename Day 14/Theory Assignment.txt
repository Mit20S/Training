2. What is CDN? What are its advantages?
CDN stands for Content Delivery Netwrok. 
It increases data access by increasing bandwidth and reducing access latency.
It is a global network of servers that work together to provide ultra-fast delivery of Internet content, such as web pages and streaming video.
